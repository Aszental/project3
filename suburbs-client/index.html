What's BurbFindr?

BurbFindr is a simple to use tool to help you find an affordable suburb to live in, in your city. With hundreds of suburbs in metro areas, even knowing where to look can be daunting. Thankfully we're here to help start the process off for you!

How do I use it?

It's dead simple to use BurbFindr. All you need to do is enter how much you'd like to spend on your property, and which area you'd like to focus on.

What will I get

We look at your criteria and match you with a list of suburb options that suit your financial position. Giving you a narrowed down set of suburb options will free you up for the fun stuff - like checking out the coffee and craft beer options around. I'm looking at you Melbournians.
